More Than a Car Wash

We're a locally-owned business dedicated to providing a fast, friendly, and high-quality wash experience.

Our Story

Fancy Car Wash was founded with a simple mission: to make it easy and affordable for the people of Milwaukee to drive a clean car every day. We saw a need for a modern, efficient car wash that didn't compromise on quality or customer service.

We invested in the latest technology, from our license plate recognition system to our gentle, closed-cell foam brushes and eco-friendly cleaning products. We're proud to be a part of the community and are committed to delivering a "fancy" clean, every time.

Our Commitment
