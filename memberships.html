Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Unlimited Wash Club work?

You pay one low monthly fee and can wash your registered vehicle once per day. Our system recognizes your license plate automatically, so you just drive up to the gate and it opens for you. It's that simple!

How do I sign up?

Click the "Join" button on your desired plan above! You'll be taken to our secure payment portal to enter your vehicle and payment information. Your plan will be active immediately.

Can I use my membership for more than one car?

Each Unlimited membership is tied to a single vehicle. However, we offer a discount for adding multiple vehicles to the same account! Please contact us for multi-car pricing.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership at any time. Simply log in to your account through the "Member Login" link on our website or contact us at least 5 days before your billing date, and we will take care of it for you. No long-term contracts!
